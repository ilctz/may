As the summer of ‘23 turns to a leafy fall, Martha May is set to deliver her forth slice of musical majesty onto the world. Following the powerful ‘Breakaway’, peppy bopper ‘Little Me’ and melancholic ballad ‘Distance’ (performed live on last year’s Croatian EuroVision National Final!), ‘Birthday Wish’ sees Martha soar into a whole new sonic landscape.
Flowering from a darker place than her previous offerings, ‘Birthday Wish’ is Martha’s most personal piece to date. Confronting the pain of body dysmorphia, May’s lyrics approach this internal struggle with her own signature sense of optimism.
Amid the sweeping strings and rich atmospherics, ‘Birthday Wish’ paints a picture of vulnerability that comes with intense self-doubt. With surface image defining us more than ever, the struggle for body acceptance can be a lonely, endless road.Yet Martha offers a soothing voice to the countless souls on this horrible journey. A voice that sings quite simply: ‘I’m right there with you!’
Inspired by the introspective works of Lorde, Olivia Rodrigo, and Orla Gartland, ‘Birthday Wish’ is Martha May as you’ve never heard her before.
Time to blow out the candles.

Hailing from the coastal city of Split, Croatia, Martha May is a heartfelt songwriter and born performer. Infusing deeply personal lyrics with an uplifting pop energy, Martha's music is sure to move you in every sense of the word.


Marking Martha's debut single and first ever written song, Breakaway is a story of self acceptance, and redefining turmoil. Created in an experimental spirit during lockdown, this uplifting banger will announce Martha's arrival in style.




Strummed during her University days on a rickety old guitar, Breakaway's emotive lyrics illustrate Martha's journey through a tough relationship. Emerging as her own woman, Martha delivers an electrifying message of emotional strenght to her fastgrowing fan base. Production-wise, Breakaway opens up with a sunny, expansive sound. Expect plenty of driving beats, warm pads and sparkling guitar lines alongside Martha's soaring vocal harmonies. Bright and optimistic, yet driving and cathartic, Breakaway is the perfect encapsulation of Martha's musical mindset.
