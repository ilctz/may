BIO

Hailing from the coastal city of Split, Croatia, Martha May is a heartfelt songwriter and born performer. Infusing deeply personal lyrics with an uplifting pop energy, Martha's music is sure to move you in every sense of the word.


Marking Martha's debut single and first ever written song, Breakaway is a story of self acceptance, and redefining turmoil. Created in an experimental spirit during lockdown, this uplifting banger will announce Martha's arrival in style.




Strummed during her University days on a rickety old guitar, Breakaway's emotive lyrics illustrate Martha's journey through a tough relationship. Emerging as her own woman, Martha delivers an electrifying message of emotional strenght to her fastgrowing fan base. Production-wise, Breakaway opens up with a sunny, expansive sound. Expect plenty of driving beats, warm pads and sparkling guitar lines alongside Martha's soaring vocal harmonies. Bright and optimistic, yet driving and cathartic, Breakaway is the perfect encapsulation of Martha's musical mindset.


Get ready to Breakaway!
